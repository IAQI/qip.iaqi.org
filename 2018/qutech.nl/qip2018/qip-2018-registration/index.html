QIP 2018 Registration

Early registration opens on September 15, 2017 (AoE Timezone) and closes on November 19, 2017 (AoE Timezone). After that the late fee will be charged.

The participation fee for includes the following: program booklet, a participants list, all sessions, tutorial days, opening reception, lunches (except on Wednesday), coffee & tea breaks, poster sessions and industry exhibit. The conference dinner is not included in the participation fee.

Registration fees 
Early regular fee € 420,00
Late regular fee € 600,00
Student fee € 300,00
Student late fee € 400,00
On site fee € 700,00
* A student is defined as a BSc, MSc or PhD student.
