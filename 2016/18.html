Tutorial Registration

Submitted by jibran.rashid on Tue, 08/25/2015 - 7:31am

Please provide the following information to register for QIP 2016 tutorial sessions. The tutorial sessions will be held at the University of Calgary in Calgary, Alberta, Canada on January 9-10, 2016. Accurate information will help us ensure correct room size for the tutorials and coffee availability during breaks. Lunch options are available at the nearby MacEwan Centre.

The deadline to reserve travel from Calgary to Banff has passed.

Complimentary transportation from Calgary to Banff is already fully booked. If you are registering late and would like your name added to the wait list, please contact Hoan Dang (hoan.dang@ucalgary.ca).
