By Taxi (Recommended)

Taxis will stop at taxi pick-up points at the arrival levels. The journey to Capella Singapore, Sentosa is estimated to cost about SGD 30.00 and takes approximately 35 minutes.

By Train (MRT)

Singapore Changi Airport MRT Station is located under Terminals 2 and 3, and is linked to the arrival and departure halls by escalators and lifts. The first train arrives at around 0530hr and the last leaves at 2318hr.

You would need to board the train from Singapore Changi Airport Station. Alight at Tanah Merah Station and transfer to another train of the East-West Line in the direction to Joo Koon. Alight at Outram Park Station and transfer to the train of the North-East Line to the Harbour Front MRT Station. From there, you can go to the 3rd level of the Vivo City shopping mall, transfer onto Sentosa Express and alight at the Beach Station, then take the Yellow Bus.

A single trip fare from Singapore Changi Airport to Sentosa costs less than SGD 6 and takes approximately 75 minutes. Tickets are available at the station. For more information, please visit Singapore Mass Rapid Transit. http://www.smrt.com.sg/trains/trains.asp
