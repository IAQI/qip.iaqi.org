Tutorial Programme - Venue

How to get to NUS (the tutorial venue) from Sentosa/Vivo City/Harbourfront

By Taxi:

A taxi from Sentosa should cost you about 10 SGD. Tell the driver to take you to NUS, Lower Kent Ridge Road, Science Drive 4.

By public transport:

Take the Sentosa Express from Sentosa, which will stop in the Vivo City mall on the third floor. Take the escalators down to the first floor. If you walk straight for a few metre and then turn right you should see signs pointing towards the bus stop and to the taxi stand nearby (a taxi from there should cost you around 8 SGD, see below for instructions).

Alternatively, use the orange Sentosa bus, which takes you to the Harbourfront bus stop.

From the bus stop take bus 97 (which should leave every 15 min), but avoid the express bus 97e. The bus stop where you should get out is 'Opposite Dover Institute of Technical Education, AYE', which is 13 stops after Vivo City and 12 stops after Harbourfront (see the map below). From there it is a short walk to the tutorial venue LT32.

On Foot:

Take the cable car from Sentosa to Mount Faber (or the Sentosa Express to Vivo City). From there you can walk all the way to NUS in a system of interconnected parks, with canopy walks and interesting sites. For more details see here .

The whole walk is about 10 km. Bring lots of water. Don't feed the monkeys.
