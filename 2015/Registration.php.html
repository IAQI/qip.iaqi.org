Substitution/Cancellation Policy
In case a registered attendee is unable to attend the conference, s/he may send a substitute to attend the conference. In this case, we require the attendee to inform the conference coordinator before January 1, 2015.

Cancellation request must be made to the conference coordinator (qip2015@quantum-lab.org) in writing.

Before October 31, 2014, refunds can be issued subject to 20% administration fee.

During November 1-30, 2014, 50% refunds of the registration fee can be issued.

After December 1, 2014, no refunds will be made for cancellation.

Please note that the day we receive your notice will be considered as the day of your cancellation, and all refunds will be made after the conference. Bank charges for cancellation refunds will be the responsibility of attendees.
