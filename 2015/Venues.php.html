Conference Banquet 14 Jan 2015

The conference banquet will be held on the evening of Wednesday January 14. This year’s QIP banquet will be held on a 3 hour-long cruise around Sydney Harbour on the “Sydney Showboat II”. The boat will depart Wharf 5 at the King Street Wharves at 7:30 PM.

King Street Wharf 5 is a 30 minute walk from the main conference venue. Simply follow signs to China Town and Darling Harbour and then walk along the Eastern side (or right-hand side in the direction of travel) of Darling Harbour until you reach the wharf. Alternately, you can get to the wharf by train (Town Hall stop) or by taxi.
