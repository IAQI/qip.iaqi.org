QIP Workshop
30 January - 3 February 2007
Brisbane, Queensland, Australia

 

Subject matter: QIP covers theoretical aspects of quantum information science,
including quantum computing, quantum cryptography, and quantum information theory.

Format: This year's program will follow a similar format to QIP 2006 (Paris),
with approximately 10 invited talks, and 30 contributed talks.

Contributed talks: The deadline for abstract submission IS NOW CLOSED. QIP contributors will be informed of the outcome of their abstract submission by 12 December.

REGISTRATION HAS CLOSED! Due to high demand, we have reached our limit of 200 attendees. Speakers at QIP who failed to register should contact the organizers immediately.
