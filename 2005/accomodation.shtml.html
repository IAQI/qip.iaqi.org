 		Accomodation


We have rooms on hold from Jan 13-16 at two hotels, the Cambridge Marriott (phone: 617-494-6600) and the Cambridge Residence Inn (phone: 617-349-0700). Both hotels are very close to both the conference site and the Kendall Square subway station. We have rooms held at each hotel for the discounted rate of $139/night. To get this rate, make your reservation by Dec 15, 2004 and mention that you are here for the QIP 2005 conference.
