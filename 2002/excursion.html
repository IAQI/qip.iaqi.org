Excursion Tuesday Night 15 Jan to the Broadway play

Proof

8PM,  Walter Kerr Theater,  219 W 48 St.

We will organize transportation to Tarrytown Station, from which you can take the 5:47 PM train arriving in Grand Central Terminal at 6:33 PM.  It is about a 20 minute walk to the theater (see Map ) or in bad weather you can take a taxi.   Probably the most convenient place to have dinner is in one of the restaurants in the recently restored Grand Central Terminal, which is a New York landmark worth exploring in its own right.  Restaurants range from formal ones like the Oyster Bar, requiring reservations, to eat-and-go sandwich shops.  After the play, trains back to Tarrytown leave Grand Central at 10:20, 11:02, 11:20, 11:58 PM , 12:20AM and 1:20AM.

Attendees and guests should purchase their own tickets.

Tickets can be purchased online through Telecharge.com

For maximum availability, order your tickets early.
