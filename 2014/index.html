Quantum Information Processing (QIP) is a rapidly developing field of research spanning both physics and computer science. As the name implies, the field extends information processing (including computing and cryptography) to physical regimes where quantum effects become significant.

QIP 2014 is the seventeenth conference on theoretical aspects of quantum computing, quantum cryptography, and quantum information, in a series that started in Aarhus in 1998 and was last held in Beijing in 2013. The conference will take place between February 03 and February 07, 2014. QIP 2014 will feature invited talks, contributed talks, and a poster session. In addition, there will be a rump session with short informal talks.

QIP 2014 will be the first edition of the QIP conference in Spain. It will be hosted by the Autonomous University of Barcelona (UAB), the University of Barcelona (UB) and the Institute for Photonic Sciences (ICFO). The venue will be at AXA Auditorium in Barcelona.
