Welcome to join QIP2024!!

The international conference on Quantum Information Processing (QIP) is the premier annual meeting for quantum information research. Since the first meeting in Aarhus (Denmark) in 1998, the conference has featured breakthroughs by leaders in the disciplines of computing, cryptography, information theory, mathematics, and physics.

The scientific objective of the series is to gather the theoretical quantum information community to present and discuss the latest groundbreaking work in the field.


Date: January 13-19, 2024

Venue: Taipei International Convention Center (TICC), Taipei 

Invited Speakers: (more to be confirmed)

  • Anurag Anshu (Harvard University)
  • Dolev Bluvstein (Harvard University)
  • Fernando Brandão (AWS and Caltech)
  • Anne Broadbent (University of Ottawa)
  • Garnet Chan (Caltech)
  • Robin Kothari (Google Quantum AI)
