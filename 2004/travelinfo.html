Airways Transit

Airways Transit provides shared ride door to door service between Kitchener-Waterloo and the Toronto and Hamilton airports.

ADVANCE RESERVATIONS ARE REQUIRED FOR ALL SERVICES.

For Toronto airport transfers we have obtained a reduced conference rate of $38.00 per person, one way, tax included. Please identify yourself as a QIP 2004 delegate when making your reservation to receive this reduced fare. There is no conference rate available for Hamilton airport.

To book transfers to/from the Pearson International Airport in Toronto, Ontario:

Online: www.airwaystransit.com
By phone: (519) 886-2121 (24 hours)

To book transfers to/from John C. Munro Hamilton International Airport

By phone only: (519)886-2121 (24 hours)

Prepayment by credit card is required at time of booking. No shows are charged at full fare and cancellations made less than 12 hours prior to flight arrival or less than 12 hours prior to pick-up time will be charged at full fare. Hamilton fares: 1 passenger: $63.00, 2 passengers: $87.00.

All fares are in Canadian dollars, cover one way, tax included and are subject to change for reservations booked after December 31, 2003.

Grand River Transit (585-2370) operate buses throughout the cities of Kitchener, Waterloo and Cambridge on a fixed route schedule. There are direct buses from uptown Waterloo (King Street) to Waterloo Inn. Click here to see a bus schedule for route 7. To get between Waterloo Inn and the conference site, 7C is the appropriate route. The stops closest to Waterloo Inn are 1319 and 1325 (on King, just before Hwy 85); the stops closest to the conference site are 1300 and 1312 (the intersection of King and Erb). Fares are CAD$2 and are good for 60 minutes of travel (ask the bus driver for a transfer slip). You may pay the driver or purchase tickets at reduced fares at the Kitchener Bus Terminal, 15 Charles Street West, in Kitchener, or at the Customer Service Desk in Conestoga Mall.
