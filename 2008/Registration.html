Registration


If you cannot access the registration server, please try again after a few hours. The connection to the server been flaky recently. Sorry.

You can register electronically by following this link: Registration Server.
This server helps you register for QIP 2008 as well as FSTTCS 2007 and its satellite workshops. Information about what form to fill out and how to compute your registration fees are mentioned there. For most participants, the registration process will involve two steps.

Step 1: creating your account. First, fill out a form for new registrants. In this form you do not have to enter the registration fees or the mode of payment. The web site will send you an email (the sender will be fsttcs07) acknowledging your request for registration.

Step 2: paying the fees and updating your information. Consult the Registration Server to find out how much you must pay and how. If you are registering for FSTTCS 2007 as well, then you can make a single payment for the total amount. After you have made the payment use the form for updating information (you will need your password) to enter the payment details (mode of transfer, date, bank etc.). We will confirm your registration by email as soon as we receive your payment. The registration fee is not refundable. Also, to avail of the early registration rates your payment must reach us by 15 November 2007. QIP 2008 is not offering any registration discounts. We might be able to reimburse the travel costs of a limited number of participants, so please save your tickets, if you wish to apply for this.

Corresponding with the organisers. After your account has been created, the preferred means of corresponding with the organisers is through the registration server.
