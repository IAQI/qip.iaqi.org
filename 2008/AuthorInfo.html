Call for submissions: [PDF] [text].

Submission of abstracts for contributed talks is now closed.

Submissions of abstracts for contributed posters can be made at the

SUBMISSION SERVER

and will be accepted until 23:59 EDT, NOVEMBER 15, 2007. Submissions are sought in research areas related to quantum information science and quantum information processing. All submissions will be judged primarily by the content of their first TWO PAGES. Therefore, these two pages should clearly describe the ideas, results, techniques, and make a comparison with previous related work. Beyond the first two pages, more details can be provided; however, any material beyond the first two pages may be ignored at the discretion of the Program Committee.
