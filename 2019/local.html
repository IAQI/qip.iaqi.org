Travel information

For international travelers, CU Boulder is best reached by flying into Denver airport, which is served by most major airlines, including direct connections from London, Paris, Frankfurt, Munich, Tokyo, etc. Since Denver is a (domestic) United hub, you can usually reach it from any major airport with just one lay-over by flying into either East coast (e.g., New York City, Washington DC, Chicago), West coast (e.g., Los Angeles, San Francisco), or one of the Texas airports (Houston, Dallas).

Getting to CU Boulder from Denver airport

The best way to reach CU Boulder from Denver airport is to take the bus route AB that leaves directly from the bus terminal beneath the airport (at the airport, leave through the exit Westin Hotel/Transit Center and take the escalators down). The AB line directly serves the CU Boulder campus with stops at Broadway/Regent Dr, Broadway/Euclid Ave (preferred stop to reach the University Memorial Center where the conference takes place), and Broadway/Pleasant St. The total driving time is about 1h, and a one-way ticket costs $10.50.

Venues

Here you can find information about the venue of the Conference, the Tutorials, the poster sessions, the Conference dinner, and the Rump session.

Below is a map in which these venues are marked, together with a selection of hotels and lunch & dinner options.

Conference

The main conference venue is the University Memorial Center (UMC) located on the CU Boulder campus and marked in the map below.

Tutorials

The tutorials on Saturday, Jan 12 and Sunday, Jan 13, will take place in the lecture room G1B30 in the Physics department of CU Boulder located on campus and marked in the map below.

Poster sessions

The poster sessions will take place on Monday, January 14, 18.00 - 19.30, and Tuesday, January 15, 15.30 - 17.00, at the Byron R. White Club at Folsom Field. Folsom Field is the home stadium of the Colorado Buffaloes, and located on CU campus. The Club is a short 10min walk from the conference venue.

Conference Dinner & Welcome reception

The conference dinner on Wednesday, Jan 16, and the welcome reception on Sunday, Jan 13, will take place at the Embassy Suites by Hilton Boulder in Boulder (see the map below).

Rump session

The rump session on Thursday, Jan 17, will take place at the Rembrandt Yard Art Gallery & Event Center in Downtown Boulder (see the map below).
